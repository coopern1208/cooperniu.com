About


I am a PhD candidate in the department of physics at Brown University working on AI for theoretical physics . I am particularly interested in building AI models that can reason mathematically. My current research aims at building foundation models for theoretical physics. In physics, my research spans the various interfaces of cosmology, astrophysics, and particle physics. At Brown, I am advised by Prof. Stephon Alexander and Prof. Loukas Gouskos. I received my B.S. in Physics and Philosophy from the College of William & Mary.

Oustside of research, I am a cinephile, a jazz pianist, and a rock climber.
